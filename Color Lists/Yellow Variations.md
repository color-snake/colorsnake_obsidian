# Yellow Variations

Tints of Yellow
A tint is created by mixing white to any pure color
```palette
#FFFF00
#ffff33
#ffff66
#ffff99
#ffffcc
#FFFFFF
```

- HSL 60°, 100, 50
- HSL 60°, 100, 60
- HSL 60°, 100, 70
- HSL 60°, 100, 80
- HSL 60°, 100, 90
- HSL 60°, 100, 100

---

Shades of Yellow
A shade is achieved by adding black to any pure hue

```palette
#FFFF00
#cccc00
#999900
#666600
#333300
#000000
```



- HSL 60°, 100, 50
- HSL 60°, 100, 40
- HSL 60°, 100, 30
- HSL 60°, 100, 20
- HSL 60°, 100, 10
- HSL 60°, 100, 0
---

Tones of Yellow
A tone is produced by adding gray to any pure hue.

```palette
#FFFF00
#e6e619
#cccc33
#b3b34d
#999966
#808080
```


- HSL 60°, 100, 50
- HSL 60°, 80, 50
- HSL 60°, 60, 50
- HSL 60°, 40, 50
- HSL 60°, 20, 50
- HSL 60°, 0, 50
